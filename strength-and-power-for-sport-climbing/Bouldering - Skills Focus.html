Details

Yes, you can boulder. No, you shouldn’t be trying to send your hardest-ever. Bouldering should be skill-focused this month and ideally would see you focused on anti-style problems and cleaning up movement errors rather than chasing grades. Climb for no more than 45-60 minutes after warm-up or as indicated in today's notes.


The Workout:

  1. Start with a good general warmup (Efficient Warm up is a good example) Don't count this in session time.
  2. 15-minute Bouldering Ladder
  3. 45-60 (or as indicated in notes. minutes of open bouldering on flash level (and below) and anti-style boulders using some of the included skills drills. Rest well between boulders so fatigue does not affect your movement quality.
